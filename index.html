Thousands of Lights shines at the Capitol Hill

Last Novermber 30, 2018, The Provincial Government of La Union welcomes the Season of Christmas through "Pasko sa Kapitolyo 2018". They conducted a Lighting Ceremony at the Capitol Hill for the Opening of the Christmas Village. The place was covered with thousands of Christmas Lights that makes the place stands out and shine. Hundreds of people came to witness the beauty of the place. It is a perfect place for you to bond wtih your family.
